Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Green Peel Bio Microneedling

Is microneedling painful?

Microneedling causes minimal discomfort. A numbing cream is applied before treatment to ensure your comfort. Most clients describe the sensation as a gentle prickling or mild vibration. The needles are very fine and create only superficial micro-injuries to stimulate healing.

How long does microneedling take?

A typical microneedling session takes 30-45 minutes, including application of numbing cream and post-care. The actual treatment usually lasts 20-30 minutes depending on the area being treated and customization.

What results can I expect from microneedling?

Microneedling improves skin texture, reduces fine lines and wrinkles, minimizes pores, fades acne scars, and enhances overall skin radiance. Results appear gradually over 4-6 weeks as collagen production increases. Multiple sessions yield progressive improvements.

Is there downtime after microneedling?

Microneedling has minimal downtime. You may experience slight redness and mild swelling for 24-48 hours post-treatment, similar to a light sunburn. Most clients can return to normal activities immediately. Avoid makeup for 24 hours and intense exercise for 48 hours.

How many microneedling sessions do I need?

For optimal results, we recommend a series of 3-6 sessions spaced 4-6 weeks apart. The exact number depends on your skin concerns and goals. After your initial course, maintenance treatments every 2-3 months help sustain results.

Can microneedling treat acne scars?

Yes! Microneedling is highly effective for treating acne scars, especially atrophic (depressed) scars. It stimulates collagen production which helps fill in scarred areas and improve skin texture. Deeper scars may require more sessions or combination treatments for optimal results.

Is microneedling safe for all skin types?

Microneedling is generally safe for all skin types, including darker skin. The treatment is customizable to your specific skin condition. However, certain skin conditions (like active infections or bleeding disorders) may require precautions. We'll assess your skin during consultation.

What aftercare is needed?

Keep skin clean and moisturized. Use a gentle cleanser and avoid makeup for 24 hours. Apply SPF 30+ daily for at least 2 weeks. Avoid heat, harsh skincare products, and strenuous exercise for 48 hours. Your therapist will provide detailed aftercare instructions.
