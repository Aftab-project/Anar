Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our treatments, procedures, aftercare, and booking process.

How long do results typically last?

Results vary by treatment type. HIFU treatments typically last 6-12 months, while microneedling benefits can be seen progressively over 3-6 months and improve over time with maintenance sessions. We recommend touch-up treatments to maintain optimal results.

Is there any downtime required?

Most of our treatments are non-invasive with minimal to no downtime. You can usually return to your daily activities immediately. Some treatments may have mild temporary redness that typically subsides within a few hours to a few days.

Are the treatments safe?

Yes. All our treatments use FDA-cleared technology and are performed by trained professionals. We conduct thorough consultations to ensure treatments are suitable for your skin type and condition.

When will I see results?

Some results are immediate (like hydration from facials), while others develop gradually. Collagen-stimulating treatments show progressive improvement over 4-12 weeks as new collagen forms. We'll discuss expected timelines during your consultation.

How do I prepare for my appointment?

Please arrive with clean skin free of makeup and skincare products. Avoid sun exposure 48 hours before treatment if possible. We'll provide detailed pre- and post-treatment instructions via email after booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made with less notice may incur a cancellation fee. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

Can I combine multiple treatments?

Yes, many treatments can be combined for enhanced results. However, some combinations require spacing between sessions. Our professionals will recommend the best treatment plan during your consultation.
