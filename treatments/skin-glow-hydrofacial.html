About This Treatment

The Hydrofacial is an advanced, machine-assisted treatment that deeply cleanses, exfoliates, and hydrates the skin while infusing targeted serums for optimal results. Using innovative vortex technology and gentle suction, impurities are removed as the skin is nourished with antioxidants, peptides, and hyaluronic acid.

Suitable for all skin types, this treatment improves skin texture, reduces congestion, and restores hydration for a smoother, more radiant complexion. LED therapy is incorporated to calm redness, soften fine lines, and revitalise tired skin, leaving the face refreshed, plump, and glowing with no downtime.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Skin Glow: HydroFacial

What is the best facial for my skin type?

The ideal facial depends on your specific skin concerns and type. HydroFacial works for all skin types and focuses on hydration. BioRePeel is excellent for brightening and renewal. Korean Facials provide multi-step hydration and glow. During consultation, we'll recommend the best treatment for your goals.

How long does a facial take?

Most facials take 45-75 minutes depending on the type and any add-ons. This includes consultation, treatment, and personalized skincare advice. We recommend arriving 5-10 minutes early for check-in.

Will a facial aggravate my acne?

Professional facials are designed to improve acne-prone skin, not aggravate it. Our facials include gentle exfoliation and extraction that help clear congestion without irritating active breakouts. We avoid aggressive treatments and use appropriate products for sensitive, acne-prone skin.

When will I see results?

You'll notice immediate results with radiant, hydrated skin right after treatment. The glow typically lasts 3-7 days. For cumulative benefits (clearer skin, fewer breakouts, improved texture), we recommend regular treatments every 4-6 weeks.

Is there downtime after a facial?

No downtime! You can resume normal activities and apply makeup immediately after treatment (though we recommend waiting 24 hours). Your skin may appear slightly flushed for a few hours, which is normal and part of the healing process.

How often should I get a facial?

For maintenance, we recommend facials every 4-6 weeks to keep skin healthy and glowing. If you have specific concerns like acne or aging, more frequent treatments (every 2-4 weeks) may be beneficial. Your therapist will create a personalized plan.

What skincare should I use after a facial?

Use gentle, hydrating products for 48 hours after treatment. Avoid harsh actives, retinoids, and vitamin C serums for 24-48 hours. Apply sunscreen daily (SPF 30+). Your therapist will recommend specific products and provide detailed aftercare instructions.

Can facials help with sensitive skin?

Yes! We offer specialized facials for sensitive skin using gentle techniques and soothing products. Our therapists are trained to customize treatments to avoid irritation while still delivering benefits like hydration, calming, and gentle exfoliation.
