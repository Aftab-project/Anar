About This Treatment

Experience the ultimate non-surgical lift with HIFU Face, combining four advanced technologies - HIFU, MPT, MFU, and EMS - in one treatment to tighten, lift, and sculpt sagging skin.

This approach stimulates collagen, improves elasticity, and restores youthful contours across cheeks, jawline, and mid-face with minimal downtime.

Perfect for dramatic lifting, anti-sagging, and contouring without surgery, leaving skin visibly lifted, sculpted, and rejuvenated.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HIFU Face

Is HIFU treatment painful?

Most clients experience only mild warmth or tingling sensations during treatment. The sensation is often described as comfortable, with intensity easily adjustable to your tolerance level. No anesthesia is required, and the treatment is well-tolerated by clients of all pain sensitivities.

How long does a HIFU treatment take?

A complete HIFU treatment takes approximately 45-60 minutes, including the initial consultation, facial mapping, the treatment itself, and post-care instructions. We recommend allowing up to 60 minutes from arrival to departure for a comfortable experience.

When will I see results from HIFU?

You'll notice an immediate lifted feeling and slight firmness right after your first treatment. However, the most impressive results appear gradually as collagen remodeling continues over 2-3 months. Many clients see their best results around 8-12 weeks post-treatment as the skin continues to improve and tighten naturally.

Is there downtime after HIFU?

HIFU has minimal to no downtime. You can resume all normal activities immediately after treatment. Some clients may experience slight redness or mild swelling which typically subsides within a few hours. We recommend avoiding intense heat (saunas, hot showers) and strenuous exercise for 24 hours post-treatment to allow the skin to settle.

How long do HIFU results last?

Results from a complete HIFU course typically last 6-12 months, with many clients seeing sustained improvement for up to 18 months. Results gradually diminish as natural aging processes continue. Maintenance treatments every 4-6 months help preserve your lifted, contoured appearance and continue collagen stimulation.

Who is a good candidate for HIFU?

HIFU is suitable for most skin types and ages. It works best for clients with mild to moderate skin laxity (sagging) who want to avoid surgery. The treatment is effective for clients aged 30+, though younger clients with early signs of aging can benefit too. During your consultation, our therapist will assess your skin condition and discuss whether HIFU is the ideal choice for your goals.

What areas does HIFU treat?

HIFU effectively lifts and tightens targeted areas depending on the treatment type. HIFU Face lifts cheeks, jawline, and mid-face. HIFU Body treatments target arms, thighs, abdomen, and other areas for skin tightening and firmness. The treatment can be customized to focus on your specific concerns.

Can I combine HIFU with other treatments?

Yes! HIFU pairs beautifully with other treatments. Many clients combine it with HydroFacials, microneedling, or other non-invasive treatments for enhanced results. However, we recommend spacing treatments 1-2 weeks apart to allow your skin to fully respond to each treatment. Our therapists can recommend a personalized treatment plan.

What should I avoid before and after HIFU?

Before: Avoid excessive sun exposure, intense skin treatments, and blood-thinning supplements for 1 week before treatment. After: Avoid intense heat (saunas, hot yoga), strenuous exercise, and direct sun exposure for 24 hours. Apply sunscreen daily. Avoid aggressive skincare or acids for 48 hours post-treatment to let your skin settle.
